The Best Carvel Icream Flavors

Top 3 best icecream flavors

  1. Chocalate
  2. Cake Mix
  3. Chocolate Chip

But why these flavors?

Chocolate

Chocolate is so rich in flavor and you feel like you can eat it forever. It's not to sweet, it's not to bitter, and it goes well with many other flavors of icecream. It's a classic for a reason ;)

Cake Mix

Cake mix tastes exactly like what it's called. This icecream is sweet just enough that you feel like you want more. If you have no cake at a birthday party, no problem just get cake mix icecream. You will love the yummy flavor and will be refreashed at the same time.

Chocolate Chip

Chocolate chip will allow you to dive into the world of two delicious flavors. You will have the luck to tase the smooth vanilla flavor, while being suprised by the chocolaty flavor of the chocolate chips. It's a great two for one deal.
